Parent GuidesThe Forest

Draft eight-week outline

Sample arc for the planned pilot—subject to change. Not enrollment or a live schedule.

  1. Week 1

    What stays private?

    Name the family information that should stay at home.

    Mission: Short family information audit

  2. Week 2

    Ask before sharing

    Practice consent before posting photos or updates.

    Mission: Decide together on one family photo share

  3. Week 3

    Digital footprints

    See how names and posts leave traces online.

    Mission: Search your family name together

  4. Week 4

    App permissions

    Review what one app can access on a device.

    Mission: Audit one app as a family

  5. Week 5

    Uncomfortable contact

    Agree on a calm signal when something online feels wrong.

    Mission: Create your family stranger signal

  6. Week 6

    Password safety

    Strengthen one account the whole family relies on.

    Mission: Update one shared family password

  7. Week 7

    Digital kindness

    Model respectful messages children can copy.

    Mission: Send one kind digital message

  8. Week 8

    Trusted adults

    Name safe grown-ups children can tell without shame.

    Mission: Sign a Family Privacy Agreement
