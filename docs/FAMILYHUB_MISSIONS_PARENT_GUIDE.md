# PandaGarde Family Hub — Parent & Guardian Guide (one page)

**Print-friendly overview · 18 privacy missions · Ages 5–17 · ~5–18 min each**

Family Hub turns everyday online risks into short **family missions**: a real situation, a quick activity, a conversation, and one action on your phone or account. Progress stays on your device.

---

## What you get (in one sentence)

**Practice → agree as a family → do one real fix** — not a lecture, not a game alone.

| Step | What happens |
|------|----------------|
| 1. Intro | Real-world scenario (game sign-up, scam DM, party photo, etc.) |
| 2. Learn / Play | Age-appropriate activity or simulator |
| 3. Family talk | Prompts and a shared rule or list |
| 4. Done | One concrete next step (settings, password, opt-out, house rule) |

---

## Ages 5–8 · Little Explorers (6 missions)

| Mission | When life hits | What your family gains | Do this after |
|---------|----------------|------------------------|---------------|
| Pack Your Digital Backpack | Game asks for name, school, address | **Share / ask first / keep private** before forms | Check one app’s sign-up questions |
| Traffic Light: Safe or Not? | “You won a prize — send your address” | **Red / yellow / green** language to pause | Reminder near the family device |
| Secret Keeper Club | Friend wants your tablet password | Passwords = **house keys**, not for friends | Review one child password |
| Who Can I Talk To Online? | Stranger in chat asks where you live | **Log off, tell a trusted adult** | Save a “safe grown-ups” list |
| My Public & Private Collage | School site asks what to publish | **Footprint** starts with small public facts | Hide one profile detail |
| Permission Please! | Posting a photo of child + friend | **Ask before posting**; respect “no” | One family photo-sharing rule |

## Ages 9–12 · Privacy Detectives (6 missions)

| Mission | When life hits | What your family gains | Do this after |
|---------|----------------|------------------------|---------------|
| Digital Footprint Trail | Years on Roblox/YouTube — what do they know? | Apps **build profiles over time** | Open one app’s privacy settings |
| Phishing Patrol | “Free Robux — log in here” | **Don’t trust urgent prize links** | Open sites directly, not from DMs |
| App Permission Inspector | Game wants location + contacts + mic | **Challenge excessive permissions** | Turn off one unneeded permission |
| Privacy Settings Pro | First Instagram / social account | **Private, safer defaults** before posting | Monthly 10-min settings review |
| Screenshot Safety Challenge | Funny screenshot in class group chat | **Pause before forwarding**; anti-bullying | Family phrase: “Would everyone see this?” |
| Password Strength Lab | Weak school password in a breach | **Passphrases, no reuse** | Upgrade one important password |

## Ages 13–17 · Digital Citizens (6 missions)

| Mission | When life hits | What your family gains | Do this after |
|---------|----------------|------------------------|---------------|
| Password Fortress Builder | One hack, same password everywhere | **Manager + 2FA** on key accounts | Turn on 2FA; save backup codes |
| Online Reputation Audit | Boss/college Googles their name | **Know search results** before applications | Edit one public profile or result |
| Data Broker Discovery | People-search site lists your home | **Opt-out path** for brokers | Document steps for one broker |
| Privacy Rights Challenge | School app wants student data | **Rights to consent and delete** | Read one app’s data policy |
| Social Media Privacy Simulator | Party post causes school drama | **Audience, location, fallout** in safe practice | Review location-sharing settings |
| AI & Your Privacy | Essay pasted into ChatGPT | **Prompts may be public/training data** | Review AI privacy settings |

## Family Hub vs the PandaGarde website

| | **Website** | **Family Hub** |
|---|-------------|----------------|
| **Job** | Understand exposure (Digital Footprint Analysis), read guides, light games | **Daily missions**, kids roster, progress, certificates |
| **Activities** | 8 standalone games at `/activities` | **18 structured missions** with family talk + next step |
| **Best for** | First visit, assessment, planning | **Ongoing habits** after (or without) the assessment |

Works **standalone** or **after** the website privacy journey — same local-only storage, no server account required.
