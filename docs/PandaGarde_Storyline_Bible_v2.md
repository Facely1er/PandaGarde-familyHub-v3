# 🐼 PandaGarde Storyline Bible v2
## The Digital Bamboo Forest

> Children emotionally attach before they intellectually model systems.

---

# 1. Product Doctrine

The Digital Bamboo Forest is not a metaphor for the internet.

The forest *is* the internet — emotionally, behaviorally, and structurally embodied as a living world children can safely explore.

Children do not return for:
- privacy lessons
- compliance education
- safety lectures

They return for:
- belonging
- wonder
- ritual
- exploration
- companionship
- becoming someone inside the forest

The forest is the product.
