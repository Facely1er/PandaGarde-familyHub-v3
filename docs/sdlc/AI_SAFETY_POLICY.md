# AI Safety Policy

> Aligned with [Authoritative Governance Suite](../pandagarde_authoritative_sdlc_governance_suite_v_1.md) §10.3 and §16.

## Scope

Applies to all AI-assisted systems,
recommendation engines,
chat interfaces,
and educational guidance systems.

## Prohibited AI Behaviors

- Emotional manipulation
- Dependency reinforcement
- Unsafe health advice
- Political persuasion
- Commercial pressure tactics
- Unsafe interactions with minors

## Required Safeguards

- Human oversight
- Safety filtering
- Moderation review
- Context limitations
- Guardian transparency
- Logging and monitoring

## Child Interaction Rules

AI systems must:
- communicate clearly
- avoid pretending to be human
- avoid authority impersonation
- avoid collecting sensitive information

## Explainability

All AI-generated guidance must:
- be explainable
- disclose limitations
- avoid fabricated certainty
