# Child Safety Standard

> Aligned with [Authoritative Governance Suite](../pandagarde_authoritative_sdlc_governance_suite_v_1.md) §10. Complements [FAMILY_DATA_GOVERNANCE.md](./FAMILY_DATA_GOVERNANCE.md) and [AI_SAFETY_POLICY.md](./AI_SAFETY_POLICY.md).

## Purpose

This standard defines mandatory child safety controls
for all PandaGarde systems.

## Platform Requirements (Suite §10.1)

The platform must:

- minimize data collection  
- prioritize local-first behavior  
- avoid unnecessary tracking  
- avoid manipulative engagement systems  
- support parental awareness without surveillance framing  

## Core Principles

- Child-first design
- No exploitative engagement mechanisms
- No behavioral advertising
- No manipulative dark patterns
- No unsafe AI interactions
- No public child profiling

## Prohibited Features

- Infinite scroll for children
- Gambling-like reward systems
- Social popularity scoring
- Behavioral ad targeting
- Hidden data collection
- Unmoderated AI chat access

## Required Controls

- Guardian consent workflows
- Age-appropriate UX
- Content moderation
- Safe language filtering
- Screen-time safeguards
- Data minimization
- Explicit privacy explanations

## AI Restrictions

AI systems interacting with children must:
- avoid emotional manipulation
- avoid dependency creation
- avoid deceptive authority behavior
- avoid unsafe recommendations

## Incident Escalation

Any child safety incident must:
- trigger immediate review
- be documented
- be escalated to platform governance
- include remediation tracking
