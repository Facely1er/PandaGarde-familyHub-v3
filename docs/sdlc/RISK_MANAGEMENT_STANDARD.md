# Risk Management Standard

> Implements conflict resolution from [Authoritative Governance Suite](../pandagarde_authoritative_sdlc_governance_suite_v_1.md) §17–18: UX consistency, child safety, maintainability, accessibility, emotional trust, and architectural clarity override feature delivery.

## Risk Categories

- child safety
- privacy exposure
- educational integrity
- operational disruption
- AI misuse
- regulatory non-compliance

## Risk Process

- identify
- assess
- mitigate
- monitor
- review

## Risk Register Template

| ID | Description | Category | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation | Owner | Status |
|----|-------------|----------|------------|--------|------------|-------|--------|
| R-001 | Example: unsafe AI response to child | AI misuse | Medium | High | Moderation + filtering | Platform | Open |

## Review Cadence

- Quarterly governance review
- Ad-hoc review after any S1 incident
- Pre-release review for features touching child data or AI
