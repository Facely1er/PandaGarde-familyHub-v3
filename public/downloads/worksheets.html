PandaGarde Privacy Worksheets

Print this page for classroom or family reflection. Pair with pandagarde.com/activities and the Privacy Panda story.

1. Safe Sharing Reflection (Ages 5–9)

List three things that are okay to share online and three that should stay private.

2. Password Strength Planner (Ages 9–12)

Write one weak password example and one strong passphrase. Explain why the second is safer.

3. Digital Footprint Map (Ages 10–14)

Name four apps or sites you use. For each, note what data they might collect about you.

4. Our Family Privacy Rules (All ages)

Write three family rules for photos, passwords, and asking before downloading apps.
